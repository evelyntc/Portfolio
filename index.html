About Me

I'm a recent data science graduate student who loves working on projects that reflect what's going on in our world. My interest in data and analytics started once I saw how much of an impact this field has on our society.

I like working on a variety of different projects, from environmental issues to programs that can help small businesses like sentiment analysis. I've also developed an interest in data journalism and sharing what I've learned so far and sharing my data science journey with others. You can check out writing here.

On a personal note, I'm a mom to an energetic one year old girl. When I have some free time I like to read, watch movies and go out to eat with my husband.
